Hello! Myself Sandipan Dhali, a soon to be 2nd year undergrad student at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay. This website depicts a digital scrapbook of my experiences till now , from the first nervous steps on campus to the final horrors of endsem which really shaped me what I'm now. Join me as I recount the milestones, learnings, and memories of my life for the very first time here at IIT Bombay.

My Timeline

August 2024

First Day on Campus

The beginning of it all! It all started from when I left my home for the first time , left everyone's sad and loved one's heart broken.The train journey was really an emotional horror for me. Things settled out a bit when I entered the college and got busy with orientations and filling forms.Meeting new people and missing the old one both created such state in my mind to be honest I was totally neutral that day.Then setting up the luggages in H16 roamed in the campus around a bit, tried food from Cafe 92 and gulmohar . In the evening our ISMP mentors, introduced them to us and vice versa.Things got better when all of us wingies became specially my roomMate got really frank and friendly in the very first day . And finally, It felt better.

Mood Indigo / Techfest

From watching all these in YouTube videos to real life has been always a happening moment for me. Best part of college life. 2024-25

Hostel Memories

One of the first and finest thing of college life , an experience for the very first time which really is the heart of college life...Wingies and Hostel Memories. The unforgetteble four years of them and uncountable memories will really cherish my whole life. They fill the space of missing my family or old friends. Hitting allnighters , Crazy Birthday GPLs , Hanging outside in the late night, playing sports or sometimes a bit misunderstanding all combined created a such bond within ourself that we 12 creatures together can be easily spotted in the mess or in the volleyball court . The bond never to be broken.

Learnings & Growth

This is not a topic worth discussing at least for me. Still managed to maintain 8 cgpa for first year only with last night grinding for quizzes, midsem and even endsems. I'm never was that much of interested in study type of persons but I like to learn new skills other than college courses which interests me and that's it not more to mention.

I was kinda Introvert 2 years back from now , but recently hearing from my friends now you talk a lot literally make me happy and the college enviroment helped me to grow in this skill a lot.Okay, other than this , some management skills in some of the college events or fests , some skills related to extra curriculars and some other soft skills which definitely is building me brick by brick and all thanks goes to the new exposure in my college life, meeting with different peoples from different corners of India.
